I coded this website for my 3rd Quarter ICT Project. We were tasked with creating a website that raises awareness and educates people about fire prevention. I decided to include the basics of different types of fires on my website because each one has different factors and different ways to avoid it. Discussing the types and causes can help us understand how fires start in the first place, reducing our risk of experiencing one. Knowing how to put out a fire is also very important, and with different types of fires, we must be cautious because some common methods of putting it out can actually make the situation worse.
